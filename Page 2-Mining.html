Mining

______

A very important yet tedious and boring part of the game that if not done..... you will not be able to make anything in your founndry which can be found in your orbiter.Mining is a non-combat activity that allows players to excavate various minerals and ores that are used as resources in Cetus, Fortuna, or the Necralisk. It can be performed in the Plains of Eidolon, the Orb Vallis, and the Cambion Drift.

Before you c can begin mining, you must first acquire either a Nosam Cutter from Old Man Suumbaat in Cetus in echange for for Ostron standing, or a Sunpoint Plasma Drill from Smokefinger in Fortuna for Solaris United standing. you must then equip the mining tool in their Gear Wheel to use it. The sunpoint Plasma Drill is much more superior than any of the Nosam cutters availabe, furthermore it is easier to aquire earlier in the game on top of all of you dont need any ranks to purchase the drill.This drill is also upgradeable to make it silent and increase the range

The Minning Procces

Once you get the nesesary gear for mining make your way to one of the following the Plains, Vallis, or Drift, players have several ways of finding different veins.Veins really stand out visually as bright colored spots on rocks and rock formations. Red and yellow spots denote Ores, while Blue spots denote Gems. Note that not all rocks will not have the ore or gem veins on them and will not have any mineral veins. You may be thinking how am i meant to see such a small vain in such a vast open world area,once equipped a HUD should appear on the left side of the following features.

  • The left side of the HUD will display icons of rocks indicating the number of mineral deposits within the detector's range higher tiers of Cutters have an increased detectable number.
  • The right side of the HUD is equipped with a rangefinder that shows the distance to the closest detected mineral vein the higher tiers of Cutters have an increased detection range.
  • An audible repeating tick sinnilar to a metal detector will be heard if the detector is within range of a deposit. This sound will grow in pitch if the player is looking in the direction of the vein, and tick more frequently the closer player is to the source
  • When unzoomed, a faint diamond-shape object will appear over veins that are within your field of view.
  • Higher grade Cutters will also display a gemstone icon on your Minimap when you are close enough, making it much easier to find the deposit.

Now to begin the extraction you need to zoom in on the vein,once you do so you should see some heat spots(flashing blue spots),shooting your Mining tool at any of the heat spot on a deposit will begin the process.

A thermometer gauge ring will appear with boxed indicators that denote where you have to land in order to extract from the deposit. Simply hold the fire button and release when you are inside any of the indicators on the ring. Red deposits indicate Ores and Blue indicates Gems

As you can see this proccess somewhat mirros some kind of minni game,The goal is to stop firing when the colored lights reach as close to the middle of these white brackets as possible. The brackets can sometimes be moving or changing in size, presenting a challenge in hitting the desired spot. Occasionally, these brackets can also be accompanied by a second, smaller set of special narrow brackets, which players can attempt to hit in lieu of the larger brackets for additional rewards.

A progress gauge will fill up at the top of the Cutter's HUD, composed of 5 small mineral icons determining the amount of resources gained and the quality of the deposit that was just mined.You are able to stop the cutting at any time, however doing so will immediately "complete" the drilling spot that was cut into, preventing a retry of that spot, and thus reducing the quality and quantity of extracted minerals.This "completed" status is also in affect if u take damage during the cutting procces however do not fret because through expirience i have learned that if you take out your pimary or your secondary it will rest the minning progreess and you can start again! but uhh you didn't hear that from me.
