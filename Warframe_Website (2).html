What exactly is Warframe?

Warframe is free-to-play, third-person shooter that combines RPG elements with a massive online multiplayer experience. It was developed by Digital Extremes and first released on March 25, 2013.

it’s available on pretty much every platform you can think of: PlayStation 4 and 5, Nintendo Switch, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, and PC.

It’s no surprise the game’s been nominated for awards like The Game Award for Best Ongoing Game—(it’s always evolving with new updates, events, and expansions to keep players hooked!)

Within the game you play as... you guessed it! Warframes. Now you may ask what is a Warframe? A Warframe is essentially a cyborg shell, think Ironman's suit but way cooler. Below you can see a variety of the playable Warframes.

The Tenno are basically space ninjas with a mysterious past. They’re descendants of the Orokin era—a super-advanced, kind of creepy golden age on Earth, full of high-tech wonders and some messed-up experiments. The Tenno themselves were created as warriors and then put into cryosleep for who knows how long. Now they’ve woken up to a totally different universe, and every faction out there seems to want them gone or under their control.

As a Tenno, you get to wear Warframes—these insane, bio-mechanical suits with powers that range from summoning fire to controlling time. Each Warframe has its own personality, abilities, and style, so part of the fun is figuring out which suits your vibe. Plus, you’ll be doing everything from stealthy infiltration missions to all-out battles against space grunts, monstrous aliens, and otherworldly threats.

In a nutshell, the Tenno are the last traces of an ancient warrior culture, and now they’re out in the galaxy, fighting anyone and anything that stands in their way, unlocking old secrets, and just generally being the ultimate badasses of the universe.

As you can see there is somewhat a special relationship between the teno and warframe,thats because the tenno needs the Warframe to contain their overflowing amonut of power known as void energy.

If not contained or controlled the void energy would untimatley destroy the tenno from the inside out,the suit of aurmour they wear called a warframe allows the tenno to have control over this energy making it non-harmfull to them.Depending on what warframe the tenno is wearing it gives them special and unique abbilites, the abbilites vary across the different kinds of warframe you use.
